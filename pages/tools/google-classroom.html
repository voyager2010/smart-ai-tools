Google Classroom

Google Classroom is an online learning platform that helps teachers and students manage classes digitally.
Google Classroom is a free online education platform developed by Google. It helps teachers create virtual classrooms, share assignments, upload notes, manage student work, and communicate easily. Students can join classes, submit homework, access study materials, and learn from anywhere using computers or mobile devices. It is widely used in schools, colleges, and online learning environments.

Key Features

Online Classrooms

Create and manage virtual classrooms for students easily.

Assignments & Homework

Share assignments, notes, quizzes, and homework digitally.

Easy Communication

Teachers and students can communicate quickly using announcements and comments.

Google Integration

Works smoothly with Google Docs, Drive, Meet, and other Google tools.
