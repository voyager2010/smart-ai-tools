BandLab

BandLab is a free AI-powered music creation platform for recording, editing, and sharing music.
BandLab is a modern music creation app that allows users to create, record, edit, and publish music online. It is designed for musicians, beginners, and creators who want to produce professional-quality songs without expensive studio equipment. BandLab includes AI tools like voice effects, auto-tune, loops, beats, and mixing tools that make music production simple and fun.

Key Features

Music Recording

Record vocals, instruments, and sounds directly using your device easily.

AI Music Tools

Use AI-powered tools for beat making, auto-tune, and sound enhancement.

Mixing & Editing

Edit tracks, add effects, and mix audio like a professional music studio.

Cloud Studio

Save projects online and collaborate with other musicians anywhere.
