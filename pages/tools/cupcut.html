CapCut

CapCut is an AI-powered video editing tool used for creating professional social media videos.
CapCut is a popular and beginner-friendly video editing application used by creators, students, businesses, and social media influencers. It provides powerful AI tools, effects, transitions, templates, subtitles, music, and animations that help users create high-quality videos easily for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms.

Key Features

Easy Video Editing

Trim, cut, merge, and edit videos easily with beginner-friendly tools.

AI Effects & Filters

Add stylish filters, AI effects, animations, and transitions quickly.

Auto Captions

Generate subtitles automatically using AI speech recognition tools.

Social Media Export

Create and export videos perfectly for TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.
